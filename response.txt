Response: MONOPOLY is a property trading game from Parker Brothers designed for ages 8 and up, accommodating 2 to 8 players. The objective of the game is to become the wealthiest player through buying, renting, and selling properties. The game includes a gameboard, dice, tokens, houses, hotels, Chance and Community Chest cards, Title Deed cards, play money, and a Banker's tray. Players can choose to play by classic rules or use a Speed Die for a faster gameplay experience.
